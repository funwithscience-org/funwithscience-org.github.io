Kell Solar Measurement Analysis →

A measurement-by-measurement check of Corey Kell’s “Operation Resolute Time” (2020) and “Operation North American Sun” (2021–23) solar-elevation datasets — a flat-earth “45° limit” claim tested against standard globe-model solar geometry across 133 readings from Kabul and Fayetteville.

These reviews test specific empirical claims against published scientific data. We credit predictions that are genuinely prospective and verified; we flag predictions that are post-hoc relabels of already-known physics; and we separate model testability from rhetorical confidence.

Every analysis is open-source and reproducible. Source data, build pipelines, and review methodology are public.
